About

This site was originally made by Steven Brown, William Enriquez, Michelle Risucci, Isabelle Sanford, and Kosal Sieng, in the context of the USC Viterbi School of Engineering Data Analysis Boot Camp. It was later extended by Isabelle Sanford to its current form, through her Writing Workshop class at Bryn Mawr College. She is still working on the site, and is happy to talk about it with anyone who wants to. Thanks for stopping by!

The site itself is made using the Bootstrap library for web design and Plotly.js to create the graphics.
